Dangers

There are many dangers that go into playing sports some serious and others small. An I will be talking about the most common between all the sports that are similar.

concussions

In these sports a concussion is one of the most found injuries that they share. This injury occurs when the brain's equilibrium is thrown off by a hit to the head or fall. This injury will only take you out for a week or so but you do have to go through a protical to return to the sport.

Ligament tears

Ligament tears are also very common in these sports wether it be you turn the wrong way or you are lifting a heavy weight it could happen at any time. This injury is season ending taking 6-9 months recovery time after surgery.

Broken Bones

Broken bones are one of the worst things to happen while doing these activities. They sometimes can be season ending just depending on the type of break or fracture. This recovery could take weeks or months depending on what you break.
