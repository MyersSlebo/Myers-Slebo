Precautions

Each one of these sports the organizations that take part to protect and keep the athletes safe so they may be able to keep participating.

Equipment

Sports have different equipment depending on what you are playing so that the athlets stay safe for play.

Wrestling Equipment

Wrestling need a certain type of shoe so that they can walk on the matt and grip it while they wrestle. They also have head gear so that they do not get cauliflower ear and singlets so they do not choke one another with baggy clothes.

Football Equipment

Football has a lot more equipment like a helmet, shoulder pads, a girdle, and cleats. There are three purposes for all this equipment one so you don't slip, two so you don't get broken ribs or shoulders, three saves you from a concussion.

Rugby Equipment

Rugby doesn't have much except cleats and the head gear that goes with it because of the way they have you tackle is safer than football.

Powerlifting Equipment

Powerlifting doesn't have much either they have wrist, knee, and straps that make sure you don't tear ligaments or breaks bones
